Inverse Problems Seminar

The Inverse Problems Seminar series aims to encourage interaction between researchers in inverse problems, imaging, partial differential equations (PDEs) and other applied areas. It is jointly organized between HKUST Jockey Club Institute for Advanced Study, Department of Mathematics, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Department of Mathematics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Department of Mathematics, City University of Hong Kong.
